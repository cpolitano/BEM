BEM 101

  1. Consistent naming convention makes relationship between markup and styles easy to understand at a glance
  2. Blocks and elements should be as abstract as possible (ex. .media instead of .photo
  3. Everything is a class and nothing is nested.

Why BEM?


  1. Faster
  2. Maintainable
  3. Extensible
